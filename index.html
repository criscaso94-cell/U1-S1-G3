Focaccia with Caramelized onion, Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions,tart cranberries and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image:Sally Vegas

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups(500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/2 teaspons salt
  • 3/4 cups(350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil,plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oli, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie,cut into cubes/scrape rind off a paring knife if desideres,though it's not
  • 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

1. Make the focaccia dought

In a medium-size mixing bowl,or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togheter all dought ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. Yoy don't need to actually knead the dough,though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
